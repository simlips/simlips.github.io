Welcome to Sword Egg!

For centuries the Ghost of Helmstead has taken the people of Zana as its prisoners. Day in and day night the people worked hard farming and raising food, developing and crafting tier weapons, and researching new magic spells for the Dark one. One day an individual stood up tall and announced "THIS ENDS NOW! Let our people free!"

These were words Grandmother told me as she continued the tale of the might Blacksmith. I looked outside and saw pain and rage of people battling. This war has went on for a century and as Grandmother's words resonanted within me, I sought out freedom for the Zana people.
